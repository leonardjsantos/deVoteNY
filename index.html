#WE ARE

The Coalition for a Future Without Violence, a collective of NYU Grad Students with the common goal of creating a loving society.

#WE BELIEVE

A world without violence and oppression begins with education, and we have an opportunity to reach that goal with Bill S4844 / A6512 introduced in the New York Senate and Assembly. This legislation would mandate comprehensive, accurate, inclusive, and age-appropriate sexuality education in New York schools.
