About

The Coalition for a Future Without Violence is here to call on New York Residents to contact your representatives and ask them to support Bills S4844 & A6512.

About the Bills:

Bills S4844 & A6512 were created by Sen. Jen Metzger, D-Rosendale & Assemblywoman Cathy Nolan, D-Queens.

They require “comprehensive sexuality instruction for students in grades K-12 which addresses age and developmentally appropriate physical, mental, emotional and social dimensions of human sexuality and reflects the national sexuality education standards.”
