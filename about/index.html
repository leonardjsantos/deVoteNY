The Coalition for a Future Without Violence is here to call on New York Residents to contact your representatives and ask them to support Bill S4844 / A6512.

About The Bill

Bill S4844/A6512 was created by Sen. Jen Metzger, D-Rosendale & Assemblywoman Cathy Nolan, D-Queens. It is one bill with two names and processes as Bill S4844 goes through the New York State Senate, and Bill A6512 goes through the New York State Assembly.

This legislation would require “comprehensive sexuality instruction for students in grades K-12 which addresses age and developmentally appropriate physical, mental, emotional and social dimensions of human sexuality and reflects the national sexuality education standards.”

Senate Bill S4844


Assembly Bill A6512

Why We Need This Bill

New York State Does Not Mandate Sex Ed, but at least 39 states and DC mandate sex education.
