Act Now!

Contact your New York State Senate and Assembly Members to sponsor/support Bill S4844/A6512!

This Bill has to pass both the New York State Senate and New York Assembly, and then finally get signed by the New York Governor to become a law.

1. FIND

Look Up Your New York State Senator + Assemblymember Contact Information Below!

  1. Type your NY State address in the bar, then select your address from the drop-down menu
  2. Scroll down and select “State” to find your State Assemblymember and Senator
  3. Be sure to write down the phone numbers and email addresses of both your Assemblymember and Senator!

FIND YOUR REP

2. CALL

Call your Senator + Assemblymember to ask them to support Bill S4844/A6512!

Calling is the most effective form of contacting your representatives. Even if no one answers, be sure to leave a voicemail as the message will still be received! View our sample call script below:

Hi, my name is [name] and I’m calling from [city and zip code] to urge [representative’s name] to support Assembly Bill A6512 / Senate Bill S4844.

Currently, New York State does not require comprehensive sexuality education in schools, and this legislation would change that. This is important for the health, safety, and well-being of our children as well as our community at large.

I strongly support Bill A6512/S4844, I want it to be voted on, and I urge [representative’s name] to sponsor and support this legislation.

Thank you!

3. EMAIL

Email your Senator + Assemblymember to ask them to support Bill S4844/A6512!

Emails are the second most effective way to contact your representatives! Remember to send one to your Senator and one to your Assemblymember, since we want this to pass both the Senate and the Assembly! View our sample email script below:

Dear [Representative’s Name],

My name is [name] and I’m from [city and zip code]. I’m contacting you to support Assembly Bill A6512 / Senate Bill S4844.

Currently, New York State does not require comprehensive sexuality education in schools, and this legislation would change that. This is important for the health, safety, and well-being of our children as well as our community at large.

I strongly support Bill A6512/S4844, I want this legislation to be voted on, and I ask that you sponsor and support it.

Thank you!

Best,
[Your Name]

4. CONNECT WITH US

Finished contacting your reps? Thanks for taking action! Let us know if we can contact you to see how your experience went!
